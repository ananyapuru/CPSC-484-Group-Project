Improving Forward Head Posture

Forward head posture is a common issue resulting from certain daily habits. Here's what you need to know:

Causes:

  • Hunching while sitting
  • Prolonged use of electronic devices
  • Sleeping with the head raised

Symptoms:

  • Anterior neck pain and headaches
  • Trigger-sensitive areas in the neck
  • Respiratory issues

Suggested Stretches:

  • Chin tucks
  • Chest stretch
