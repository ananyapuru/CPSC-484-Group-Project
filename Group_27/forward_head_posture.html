Causes:
  • Hunching while sitting
  • Prolonged use of electronic devices
  • Sleeping with head raised
Symptoms:
  • Anterior neck pain and headaches
  • Trigger-sensitive areas
  • Respiratory issues
Remedial exercises:
  • Chin tuck exercises
  • Chest stretch exercises
